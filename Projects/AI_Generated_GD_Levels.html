AI Geometry Dash Levels

During the summer of 2025, I had an idea to create an AI that had the ability to create Geometry Dash level layouts. I started, but quickly became bored in the process. I decided to focus elsewhere because I had other responsibilities in real life at this point. Around late March 2026, I regained interest in this project. I started working on it again, but had quickly run into a roadblock. I had no way of getting more training data for this AI other than the layouts I have already made in my lifetime, and that was proving to not be enough. I had then decided to make a Reddit and YouTube post about this project. This was also the first time I had ever publically posted about this project. Of course, after I had trained this first model, I made a video about it.
